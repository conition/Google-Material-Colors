What is Google Material Colors?

Google Material Design Colors is a palette of colors inspired by everyday life, unexpected and vibrant. It was made to make sure that designers could work together and use harmonized colors. It follows Google's Material Design Guidelines. To read more, click here.

Why was this website made?

I made this website because I hated copying a pasting colors to my code from Google's website. It was frustrating and took to long. Other websites took too long to use and didn't have the speed functionality that I wanted.

With this website, I can easily get the color that I need in an instant.

How to use it

It's extremely simple to use this website. Simply tap on the primary color you would like, then select the shade you would like to copy. It will automatically be copied to your clipboard.

It's recommended to use a 500 (the color with the white border) and an accent color (One of the bottom 4 colors) and use them throughout your entire project.

To read more about harmonizing colors and what colors to use, click here.

Who made it?
